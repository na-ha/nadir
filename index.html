N1



N1 is a bag made of the creamiest and silkiest silicone sauce.

Carefully crafted to be delicate, flexible and resilient,

N1 is an incredible utility and design piece where engineering meets artistry.

N1 has a slit to slide the most essential items,

making it the perfect comapanion for any occasion.

The compartment can hold a mobile phone and other small accessories

N1 is entirely made in Paris [Intra Muros],

from European silicone [France, Germany and Italy]

and ships worldwide for free.

It currently takes about 24 hours for an artisan to make one N1.

Each step of the process requires vigorous attention to detail,

calculated risk, extreme patience and a steady hand.

Due to our current crafting processes, production is tremendously scarce,

in response to rising demand, prices go up to hedge against an incontrollable influx of orders.

Today, You can buy the N1 directly OR;

You can buy the corresponding NFT and redeem it for the physical bag at a later date.
